ABOUT US

Indala College of Engineering primarily focuses on the four major pillars of the students development; Intellectual, Spiritual, Physical, and Social. It also provides facilities for the pursuit of excellence and an academically suitable climate of the scholarship in terms of curriculum and curricular performance of the students.


The goal of the Institution is to increase the Scope and Justification of its establishment in the light of thenprevailing Technical Education and Industrial scenario in the country.
